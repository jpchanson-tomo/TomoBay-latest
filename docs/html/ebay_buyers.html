ebay_buyers

Home > Development > Design > Database > ebay_buyers

This table contains all information relating to an eBay buyer (someone who bought something off eBay). It includes all the usual address and name information as well as email and phone number if available.

  • buyerID this is the primary key of the table and the unique identifier for each record in this table. It is a VARCHAR/String field with a maximum length of 40 characters. This field is equivalent to the eBay user id of the buyer.
  • name This is pretty self explanatory, it is the real name of the buyer. It is a VARCHAR/String field with a maximum length of 45 characters.
  • street1 This is the first line of the buyers address, it is a VARCHAR/String field with a maximum length of 80 characters.
  • street2 This is the second line of the buyers address, it is a VARCHAR/String field with a maximum length of 80 characters.
  • city This is the line in the address that represents the postal city/town of the buyer, it is a VARCHAR/String field with a maximum length of 80 characters.
  • county This is the line in the address that represents the county that the buyer lives in, it is a VARCHAR/String field with a maximum length of 80 characters.
  • postcode This is the line in the address that represents the postcode that the buyer lives in , it is a VARCHAR/String field with a maximum length of 15 characters.
  • email This field represents the contact email address of the buyer, it is a VARCHAR/String field with a maximum length of 100 characters.
  • phone This field stores the contact telephone number of the buyer, it is an Int field with a maximum length of 16 digits.
